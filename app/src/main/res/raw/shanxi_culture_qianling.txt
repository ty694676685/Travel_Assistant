Qianling Tomb is one of the eighteen tombs of Tang Dynasty in Guanzhong area of Shaanxi Province. It is located on Liangshan Mountain, 6 kilometers north of Qianxian City, Xianyang City, Shaanxi Province. Liangshan is a conical limestone mountain body, with three peaks. The north peak is the highest, with an altitude of 1047.9 meters. Sihe River encircles its East and Moshui surrounds its west. The Xuangong of Qianling Tomb is located on the North peak. The two peaks in the south of Liang are relatively low, confronting the East and the west, with Sima Road in the middle. Therefore, the two peaks are named Rufeng.
