
Wang Zhihe stinky tofu is a traditional snack in old Beijing, belonging to a kind of tofu. The color is blue, smells bad and tastes good. The inventor was Wang Zhihe, an Anhui native, who has been around for more than 300 years. He was a literary man, who failed in many tests in Beijing. Instead, he made a living by making tofu and created stinky tofu. The stinky tofu was once sent to the palace as an imperial meal dish, which was loved by Empress Dowager Cixi and was named "Yuqing Fang" by herself.
