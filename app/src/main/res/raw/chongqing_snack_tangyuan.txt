
Tangyuan in Fermented Glutinous Rice Soup is a traditional feature, festival food custom. It belongs to one of dessert dessert recipes. Tangyuan is used as the main ingredient. The cooking skill of mashed grains Tangyuan is mainly cooking, and its taste is sweet. Tangyuan with mashed grains is a long-standing snack among Chinese people, which is widely popular in the Yangtze River Basin. Boil the mashed grains with water and put them into the dumplings (usually small dumplings without fillings). When the dumplings float, add a small amount of osmanthus. Steamed grains and dumplings are delicate and refreshing, with a liquor flavor but not strong. In addition, it can also be boiled into the egg, nutritional taste is better.
