
Hangzhou Dongpo Pork is a traditional dish with full color, aroma and flavor, which belongs to Zhejiang cuisine. Dongpo meat is a dish named after Su Dongpo. Su Dongpo (Su Shi) was a famous poet in the Northern Song Dynasty of China. He had a deep knowledge of poets and calligraphy, as well as a good study of cooking dishes. He can cook his own dishes, and is very good at cooking meat. In many of his famous poems, he also has something about diet.  
          
Meat crispy and delicious, fat but not greasy.
