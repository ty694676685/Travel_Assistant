
Fried sauce noodles are a distinctive food in Beijing. They are made of food codes, fried sauce and noodles. Cut or boil cucumbers, toons, bean sprouts, green beans and soybeans, and make them into dishes for reserve. Then make fried sauce, stir-fry diced meat and scallion and ginger in oil, add soybean sauce or sweet noodle sauce, fried sauce, that is, fried sauce. After the noodles are cooked, they are taken out and poured with fried sauce. The noodles are mixed with food codes to form fried sauce noodles. Some noodles are salvaged and washed in cold water, followed by fried sauce and dish size, which is called "over water".
