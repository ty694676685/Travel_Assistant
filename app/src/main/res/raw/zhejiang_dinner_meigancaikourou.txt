
Braised pork with preserved vegetable is a traditional dish in Zhejiang. After cooking the pork, put the pickled dried plum vegetable in the bowl, so that the dried plum vegetable and the spiced meat are ready. 

Preserved vegetable is a local specialty of Shaoxing. Shaoxing dried plum rape oil is dark, mellow and durable for storage. It can be divided into three types: Chinese cabbage, rape and mustard. The best type of mustard is "Brassica juncea" pickled and dried. Plum dried vegetables steamed and fried pork, vegetables contain meat oil, meat with vegetable fragrance, fat but not greasy, delicious.
