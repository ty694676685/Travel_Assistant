
Chen Ma Hua is a famous traditional snack in Chongqing. At the end of the Qing Dynasty, Chen Ma Hua, an ancient town, was spread throughout the country because of its unique taste. Because of its excellent material selection and handcrafted, Chen Ma Hua had the characteristics of fragrance, crispness, crispness and long-lasting non-cotton.
