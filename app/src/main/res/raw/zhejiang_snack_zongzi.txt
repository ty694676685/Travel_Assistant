
Jiaxing Zongzi is a traditional snack with Jiaxing characteristics in Zhejiang Province. It is famous for its waxy but not pasty, fat but not greasy, delicious, salty and sweet. Especially fresh meat dumplings are the most famous, known as "the king of dumplings". Jiaxing Zongzi is popular among tourists because of its delicious taste, convenient carrying and eating. It is called "Oriental Fast Food".
