
Shidu Scenic Area is located in the middle and upper reaches of the Juma River in Fangshan District, southwestern suburb of Beijing, 100 kilometers away from Beijing. It is the only natural scenic spot in North China characterized by karst peak forest and river valley landform. The scenic spot has beautiful scenery and picturesque scenery. It is known as "Northern Guilin". In history, from Zhangfang to Shidu, it was named after ten ferry crossings, known as "Northern Guilin" and "Northern Yangshuo".            
Shidu countryside scenery, unique. Under the dangerous peak, there is water winding. Ten ferry crossings have different scenery. Or walk along the coast, or sail on the water, intoxicated with the beautiful scenery. Forget about mountains and rivers, and never want to return.
