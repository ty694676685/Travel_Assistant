
The Qujiang Ocean World Project in Xi'an covers an area of 90 mu. It is mainly composed of the Ocean Hall, Haiyun Square and Ocean Business Club. The main building area of the core project is 18600 square meters; the total water body in the hall is about 6000 tons, and there are more than 12000 freshwater and seawater organisms designed for aquaculture. The hall is mainly composed of dolphin exhibition hall, marine science popularization hall, tropical rain forest hall, submarine tunnel, underwater Grand View Park and supporting service facilities. It fills the gap of tourism resources in Northwest China and creates the top five of the same projects in China. It has become a beautiful tourist scenic line.
