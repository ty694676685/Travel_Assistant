
Romon Universal Paradise is invested and constructed by Romon Group, a famous Chinese clothing brand enterprise. It is located in the Southern new town of Ningbo, adjacent to the southern business district of Manhattan, Ningbo. It has a construction area of 200,000 square meters and a total investment of more than 5 billion yuan. It is one of the largest urban indoor theme parks in the world. The park consists of a giant egg-shaped indoor amusement park with a height of 57.4 meters and an outdoor legendary island. It has six thematic areas: Romantic Avenue, Dreamworld, Mysterious Continent, Adventure Tour, Happy Square and Legendary Island. It has more than 30 amusement projects, more than 30 characteristic restaurants and more than 20 theme stores.
