
Cucurbit chicken is a traditional dish in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, which began in the Tang Dynasty. Serve with small butterflies, pepper and salt. The color is golden red, the skin is crisp and tender, the fragrance is mellow, the chopsticks come off to the bone. It is rich in protein and saturated fatty acids needed by human body. The traditional selection of Luji is the "Ni Ni" chicken from Sanlu village in the south of Xi'an city. This kind of chicken is kept for one year. Its net weight is about one kilogram. Its meat is fresh and tender.
