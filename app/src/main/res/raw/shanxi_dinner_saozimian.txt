
Saozi noodles are the traditional noodles and famous Xifu snacks in Northwest China. The Qishan Saozi noodles of Baoji are the most authentic. It is popular in Guanzhong Plain of Shaanxi Province and Longdong of Gansu Province.           

Saozi noodles have a long history, which contains side dishes such as tofu, eggs and so on. The method is simple. Saozi means diced meat. For Shaanxi people, the color matching of Saozi is particularly important. Yellow egg skin, black agaric, red carrot, green garlic seedlings, white tofu and other materials are good-looking and delicious.
