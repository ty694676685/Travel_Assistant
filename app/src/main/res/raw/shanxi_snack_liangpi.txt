
Liangpi, a delicacy originated in Guanzhong area of Shaanxi Province. It is prevalent in northern China. It is a rare natural green pollution-free food.            

Due to the different raw materials, production methods and regions, the taste has spicy, sweet and sour, spicy and other flavors. Liangpi has a long history. It is said that it originated from the Qin Shihuang period and has a history of more than 2,000 years.            

Liangpi is divided into rice and noodles, rice skin is more tendon, with soft and strong gluten, topped with red oil, tastes great.
