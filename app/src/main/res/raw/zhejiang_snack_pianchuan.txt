
Pianchuan is a delicious snack in Hangzhou. The ingredients of noodles are mainly composed of snow cabbage, bamboo shoots and lean meat slices, which are delicious and delicious. It has a history of more than a hundred years. It was first created by Kuiyuan Hall, an old shop in Hangzhou. Its characteristics lie in the delicacy of dumpling vegetables and bamboo shoots and the endless aftertaste after eating. A bowl of sour, salty and delicious, fresh and tasty Pienchuan is one of the most popular snacks in Hangzhou.
